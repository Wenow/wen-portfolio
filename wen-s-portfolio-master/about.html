Background

I'm Wen Ching Ow. I'm a product/interaction/UCD designer. Born in Jamaica, raised in Florida, Cayman Islands, and Chicago.

Previously, I was a graphic designer at Cushman & Wakefield for the Capital Markets Team in Boca Raton. In my role, I assisted the Brokers, Financial Analysts, and Brokerage Coordinators to develop custom deliverables such as offering memorandums, proposals, brochures, flyers, and other marketing materials.

I attended Florida Atlantic University, where I earned a Bachelor of Business Administration with dual concentrations in Management and Marketing in May 2016. After graduating, I enrolled in Nova Southeastern University's Master of Business Administration in Marketing Analytics program. While taking classes, I worked as a Graphic Design Intern for the University's Publication and Creative Services Department.

Before getting into Human-Centered Design my background was in Graphic Design, Business Development, Digital Marketing, Social Media Management, Event Management, and Planning. I'm most passionate about web and desktop products but have extensive experience across mobile and tablet too.

I'm interested in solving problems related to real estate, education, festivals, music, travel, e-commerce, transportation, and so on. When I'm not designing or studying, I enjoy swimming, rock climbing, and playing board games with my family and friends.
